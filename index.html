Brand Story

That is how it all started.

Founder Billy ran 7 kilometers on the most random day to lose weight right after quitting his 9-5 job. The idea of founding a running club ecosystem came to his mind when he consistently ran for 90 days and started seeing real results. So, he founded the © 7k Running Club along with Inji and Moogi, who resonated with his vision. That's how © 7k was born and became a reality. We haven't been building a brand. We've been building a cult that lives and breathes the © 7k lifestyle.

The © 7K Vision

Running club at the center.

Our Founders

Three people, one weirdly specific run culture.

Billy is a former corporate senior manager turned tech entrepreneur and the founder of Bnb Lab, a creative consulting agency. Known for building tech-leveraged businesses from inception to profitability.

Moogii

Co-founder, Operations Director

Our Community

Open weekly sessions. Real people. No weird gatekeeping.

As a running community, we host weekly public running sessions open to runners of all levels who want to vibe with us and experience the magic of running with like-minded individuals. Beyond our weekly runs, we occasionally form training groups to compete in races internationally. © 7k Running Club is a pioneering crew with over 500 members, reaching more than half a million people nationwide. Stay tuned, come run with us, and grow together.
